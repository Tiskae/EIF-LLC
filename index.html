How do we stand out
among others

We're proud to have so many passionate, reputable supporters who champion us to a wide audience. Our ambassadors and celebrity supporters make huge contributions to our work, supporting fundraising events and campaigns, and raising awareness of mental health in the media.

Some of our services

Assistance

We offer assistance to persons and their family in the entertainment industry who are unable to meet their needs due to circumstances beyond their control

Promotion

We promote talented up and coming artists and authors to a higher level in their career with an emphasis on anonymity and dignity

Entertainment

Whilst we help artists achieve their goals, we at the same time produce quality entertainment to the people
